Projects

NFL Predictions

After compiling historical data, we attempted to predict total points, spread, and the result of NFL games using statistical modeling. We correctly predicted the winning team with greater than 60% accuracy for each game over a four-week span in the 2021-22 NFL season.

Madden Cover

We used performance statistics to predict which player would be chosen as the cover athlete for the next season's EA Sports Madden video game. We went one step further and predicted the performance of that player for the next season. The motivation behind our research was to test the truth behind the rumored "Madden Curse".

Ultimate Frisbee

After studying the current state of statistics in Ultimate Frisbee, I developed innovative analysis methods and unique data collection techniques in hopes of enhancing the correlation between statistics and success on the field. In addition to developing new methods, new variables were created to better capture the factors to frisbee success.

WACC Analysis

Using the Weighted Average Cost of Capital methodology, I assessed the success of Amazon, Inc. expanding their business into the Healthcare IT industry. I made my conclusion after analyzing many captial structures, forecasting market activity, and estimating future movement within both the industry and the economy.

Cash Flow Analysis

After analyzing the given financial statements for an imaginary company, creating an Excel Workbook to calculate Operating Cash Flow, Capital Expenditures, and Net Working Capital, I assessed the profitability of introducing a new product using Net Present Value, Profitability Index, and Internal Rate of Return methodologies.

Diamond Pricing

Given data that stated the cut, color, clarity, carat weight, and price of hundreds of unique diamonds, I created a regression that correlated the price with the other four variables most closely. After fine-tuning the regression most precisely, I predicted the price of new diamonds given their characteristics.
